This Mission's Statement

Posted on April 19, 2023

The advent of webpage creation tools such as squarespace, wix, and wordpress has generally homogenized the web. In opposition to this, i've decided to spend my spare time creating a more unique form of expression on the internet, both as a homage and a challenge to myself, as so much of modern web design tools are suited for flat design. Taking inspiration from early to mid 2000s blogs and forums, I hope to hone my HTML and CSS skills, to allow myself to make stylized websites outside of the corporate memphis styled aesthetics of the modern web. I am actively seeking inspiration for this website. If you find some unique web artifact, feel free to email me a link at eyanaszaszko@gmail.com.

In addition to this, I have yet to create a purpose for this site. I don't particularly have any idea on what I want to do here, maybe just post a bit about my adventures and exploits in my own quiet way, just cataloging things that happened to me or things i've been interested in.

To start, I just recently finished Kamen Rider Kuuga, and have moved onto Geats which is at about episode 30 right now.
